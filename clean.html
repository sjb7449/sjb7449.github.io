Keep Clean

This group project was assigned to my team with the task of creating a Smart TV application that would assist individuals in some way. My group decided to create a product that focused on encouraging and motivating children to stay on task when completing their chores.

Overview

In our design, the user would access the app, like any other on their Smart TV. Then, they would be prompted to select from a collection of editable "Chore Presets." Each preset would contain a set list of related chores. For example, if the parent selected a "Laundry Day" preset, the chore list would include tasks such as folding clothes, running the washer, etc.

Once a preset is selected, the television would lock, until the children completed their assigned chores. Children can check off their chores underneath their correlating animal character (which they would choose in the settings section of the app). Once the children have completed their chores, the parent would have to unlock the TV by entering a password.
